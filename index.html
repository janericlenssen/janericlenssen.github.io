Research Interests

My interest lies in designing differentiable algorithms, architectures and representations for percieving and representing the 3D world. Past research includes works in the areas of graph neural networks, equivariant operators, implicit neural fields and differentiable eigendecomposition. Currently, I am interested in scalable data priors for 3D representations and reconstruction.
